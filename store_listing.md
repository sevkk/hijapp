## App Name

## Subtitle (iOS, max 30 char)

## Short Description (Play Store, max 80 char)
See how any hijab looks on you — try patterns instantly with your camera or photo.

## Full Description

Ever wondered how a hijab would look on you before buying it? HIJAPP lets you try any hijab pattern on your own photo or in real-time using your phone as a mirror.

HOW IT WORKS
Pick or photograph the hijab you're interested in. Then choose Photo Mode to see it on your saved pictures, or Mirror Mode to try it live with your camera. It's that simple.

PHOTO MODE
Upload your photo and the hijab you want to try. HIJAPP instantly shows you how that pattern looks on you — no waiting, no guessing.

MIRROR MODE
Open your camera and see the hijab pattern on you in real time. Move your head, check different angles, and capture the look with a photo or video.

SAVED TEMPLATES
Save your favorite photos as templates so you don't have to upload them every time. Label them for different occasions — everyday, work, wedding — and switch between them in seconds.

FEATURES
- Try any hijab pattern on your photo instantly
- Real-time mirror mode with live camera
- Save template photos for quick access
- Capture photos and videos of your looks
- Share results with friends and family
- Works completely offline — no internet needed
- Your photos never leave your device

PRIVACY FIRST
Your photos are processed entirely on your device. Nothing is uploaded to any server. We don't collect, store, or share your images.

HIJAPP PREMIUM
- Unlimited daily try-ons
- Unlimited saved hijabs and templates
- Video recording in mirror mode
- Ad-free experience

Download HIJAPP and never buy a hijab you haven't tried.
