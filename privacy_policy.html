HIJAPP Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 19, 2026

HIJAPP ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

Data We Do NOT Collect

HIJAPP processes all photos and images entirely on your device. Your photos are never uploaded to any server, cloud service, or third party. We do not collect, store, transmit, or have access to any of your images, camera feed, or generated results.

Data We Collect

If you create an account or subscribe to HIJAPP Premium, we collect your email address and subscription status through Firebase Authentication and Google Play / Apple App Store billing. This is the minimum required to manage your account and subscription.

We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage analytics (such as feature usage counts and crash reports) to improve the app. This data cannot be used to identify you personally.

Camera and Photo Library Access

HIJAPP requests access to your device camera and photo library solely to enable the hijab try-on features (Photo Mode and Mirror Mode). Camera and photo data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted externally.

Local Storage

Your saved template photos, recent hijab images, and app preferences are stored locally on your device using the app's sandboxed storage. This data is not accessible to other apps or to us.

Third-Party Services

HIJAPP may use the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

Firebase Authentication (Google) — for account management. Google AdMob — for displaying ads in the free version. Apple App Store / Google Play — for processing Premium subscriptions.

Children's Privacy

HIJAPP is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us immediately.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect your photos or images, there is nothing to retain or delete on our servers. Account data (email and subscription status) is retained as long as your account is active. You may request deletion at any time.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. You can delete your account and all associated data by contacting us. All locally stored data (templates, preferences) can be cleared by uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or by updating the "Last updated" date above.
